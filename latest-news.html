Flying Wind Turbines

Sunset Capital signs an Memorandum of Understanding with kiteKRAFT GmbH (www.kitekraft.de), a preeminent German company pioneer on the development of Flying Wind Turbines. Sunset will install 2 Pilot Flying Wind Turbines  at Laguna Energy Complex located in Santa Catarina, Brazil. Signing with kiteKraft, Sunset reaffirm its commitment to use the greatest technologies related to green energy production in order to build the company future path.

Green Energy Plant

 Brazil will have it's first two Gren Hydrogen electrolysis plants to be built by Sunset Capital. Annual production will start with 250,000 kg/year of green hydrogen for each plant. Each Green H2 Plant have a production capacity of 725,000 kg/year of "green H2" and will be implemented in phases according to demand.  Total investment will be around USD 20 Million. The first Green H2 Plant is expected to go live in the first half of 2022 and the second in the last quarter of 2022.  

 From this point, the 5 MW PEM (polymer electrolyte membrane) electrolyzer will start producing up to 250,000 kg of green hydrogen a year. The green hydrogen could be use by mining and steel companies for Direct Reduced Iron - DRI process or could be use as a reliable energy power for Datacenters and also for stablilization of energy grids.

 “By building Brazil’s first electrolysis plant, we are making another contribution to reducing CO2 and to meeting climate targets, whereby hydrogen is a key technology. We deliberately opted for green hydrogen production on an industrial scale as we see the potential it holds – for DRI use as well as for reducing CO2 emissions in many industrial operations”, said Rodrigo Boabaid, Sunset Co Founder.

 Sunset Capital laid out its ambitious targets back in January 2018. Sunset Capital Portfolio could be a very interesting asset for Green Energy Investment Funds and for Corporations from many hard-to-abate carbon sectors (Oil & Gas, Mining & Steel Companies, Chemical Industries…) compromised to achieve the Paris Agreement UNFCCC on climate change. By providing new technologies such as blockchain investment platform, green hydrogen and renewables (like the photovoltaic plant and wind power plants), we are ideally positioned to work to accelerate the transition towards a carbon-neutral economy, through emerging technologies and more environmentally friendly solutions, targeting off-grid areas and many industries from many hard-to-abate carbon sectors.. The successful and economic feasible implementation of these sustainable and innovative technologies will require an incentive system that extends beyond brazilian private sector.

 

 Sunset Capital is focused on advancing innovative technologies in its Brazil home market to address major challenges such as economic growth, job creation, and climate-protection measures.
