Technical Overview

ICO - Utility Token, Sunset Watt Tokens - SWT, related to 08 Renewable Energy Power Plant. In our projections we have considered the solar panels and equipment's degradation rate to have the best estimated energy production as possible

We aim to offer the utility token, Sunset Watt Token – SWT, as an innovative and modern way to finance green energy projects, to generate and distribute renewable energy, to remove investment barriers allowing access from investors anytime, anywhere in worldwide. We have everything ready to start from Brazil, beside that, and after well consolidated our new model our main idea is to expanding inland and off-grid areas in Brazil, on that promising unexplored market, before expanding to other emerging countries.

The main core of the business is to manage funds to install 41 MW of Renewable Energy (07 Solar Power Plant and 01 Mixed Solar & Wind Power Plant), 02 Green Hydrogen Plant (with a production capacity of up to 250,000 kg / year of green H2 each), 02 Datacenters. Those utility tokens, SWT, once listed on an exchange they will be used to finance the green energy production. Investors will have returns as soon as the renewable energy gets delivered, and the tokens can be withdrawn or transferred to another investor or even to the end buyer of our energy to be used as a payment for the energy consumed.

We can use our own exchanging platform to settle the utility tokens, SWT, together with the end buyers and utilize the exchange to negotiate the utility token on the market (buy/sell/transfer), our Sunset Capital Platform can serve as a settlement layer when investors want to use the Sunset Watt Tokens.

Blockchain, Ethereum, and Smart Contracts

With the proprietary smart contract capacity of the Ethereum blockchain, it will suffice to build the Sunset Capital Blockchain as a layer on the Ethereum platform. Why a subsequent Mainnet deployment can result in the medium term, it is a market fact that several projects launched on Ethereum platform have grown to become global market leaders.

For smart contract functionality, using the Ethereum platform will be a starting point that is worth highlighting. With smart contracts in their functionality clearly self-executing, they provide a transparent basis for renewable energy administration that all parties to the transaction will be comfortable with.

Regulatory agencies will be able to observe power generation and distribution trails in order to carry out their oversight functions. The Sunset utility tokens global investors will be also able to view their transaction history without avoidable bottlenecks that act against the goals of energy democratization.

Token sale and the Sunset Capital Blockchain Platform development will take place on the Ethereum Platform. We have chosen Ethereum for its popularity, functionality and security capabilities. However, the expected scale of Sunset Capital’s platform, according to the number of our potential users, represents tens of thousands of consumers, investors, and millions of transactions. This level of load currently can be handled by Ethereum.

We believe in the continuous development of Ethereum with Vitalik Buterin already having announced new promising features in Ethereum's upcoming versions. These features will address many of the present issues with the blockchain. At the same time, we are continuing our platform development, based on Ethereum. In the event of Sunset Capital outgrowing Ethereum's capabilities’ limit (presumably it can happen in a one-year period), we can decide to switch to another blockchain, which will be able to meet the needs of the project. Blockchain technologies are developing rapidly, and there are already some promising alternatives. In case of blockchain platform change, all Ethereum tokens will be migrated. Token quantity and conditions for holders will remain unchanged.

The range of use of smart contracts will embody the terms of renewable energy sale to Sunset global investors. Each consumer will be able to view the terms of the contract before signing and be able to see all data related to the green energy power plant generation and prices related to energy production.

Smart contracts have shown their efficiency in use by several projects in banking, inventory distribution and management as well as multieholder scenarios.

Land Registry

There is a requirement for land acquisition in order to be able to install a green energy power plant of a considerable size. With a landmass of 8.5-million-kilometer square, Brazil offers enough space for the development of a worthwhile green power generating project. With completed initial work on land scouting, acquiring the needed land for the project will not pose any challenges.

The charges associated with land acquisition include the purchase price, registry fees as well the cost of notary services. Land acquisition is relatively affordable in Brazil, which makes it a good incentive for green energy projects.

It's important to mention that grid connection, logistics, consumers, energy availability, weather conditions and specially taxes are different across states and areas in Brazil.

Our internal policies will make it mandatory to acquire Land for a long-term project of this size. We have evaluated the pros and cons and are confident in the supporting data that this renewable energy project development is a stable long-term investment.
