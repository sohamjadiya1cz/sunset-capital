Flying Wind Turbines

Green Energy Plant

 Brazil will have it's first two Gren Hydrogen electrolysis plants to be built by Sunset Capital. Annual production will start with 250,000 kg/year of green hydrogen for each plant. Each Green H2 Plant have a production capacity of 725,000 kg/year of "green H2" and will be implemented in phases according to demand.  Total investment will be around USD 20 Million. The first Green H2 Plant is expected to go live in the first half of 2022 and the second in the last quarter of 2022.
